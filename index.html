About Our DEI Commitment
Updated Q1 2025
Active

At Meridian, we believe that diverse teams build better products and stronger communities. Our Inclusion & Belonging initiatives are embedded across the employee lifecycle — from recruitment and onboarding through promotion and leadership development.

Our DEI efforts are coordinated by the Office of Inclusion, which partners with HR, Legal, and senior leadership to set goals, track progress, and resource programs across all business units.

Programs & Training

Learning and development resources for an inclusive workplace

Current Programs
Inclusive Leadership Certification Required — Managers
A 6-module course covering unconscious bias, equitable performance evaluation, and inclusive team practices. Completed by 89% of people managers in 2024.
Sponsorship Program Active cohort
12-month structured sponsorship pairing senior leaders with high-potential employees from underrepresented groups. Current cohort: 48 participants.
Equity in Hiring Workshop Quarterly
Half-day facilitated session for all hiring managers covering structured interviewing, diverse slate requirements, and bias-resistant evaluation rubrics.
Allyship in Action Open enrollment
Self-paced online course available to all employees on bystander intervention, microaggression awareness, and active allyship behaviors.

Employee Resource Groups

Community, connection, and advocacy across Meridian

ERG Leadership & Governance

Each ERG at Meridian is co-led by elected employee officers and an executive sponsor from senior leadership. ERGs receive annual budgets, dedicated meeting time, and direct access to HR and the Office of Inclusion to raise issues and shape policy.

In 2024, ERG recommendations directly influenced changes to our parental leave policy, accessibility accommodations process, and recruitment partnerships with HBCUs and Hispanic-Serving Institutions.
